Cafe Creama Grama is a combination of different things; coffee, ice cream, cake and board games.It is a place for many to socialize and rest through it's delicacies and games. It is for the people who would like to experience the thrill of chancing their luck on a dice, making and developing a character, going through an adventure and making strategies through out the different board games it offers. This establishment is owned by the Collizion Company, a private company that caters to the rich, poor and the board game players.

Collizion is an organization that specializes in funding and finding new entereprenuers that hope to open their own business. This company strives to be the starting point of every fresh entreprenuer that has the potential to be a great and well-known business owner. This company was named after the word "Collision", the event in which two or more bodies exert forces on each other in about a relatively short time. This company is a collision of many ideas and journeys, the different paths a person can take through this company inspires its workers to be more creative and productive.
