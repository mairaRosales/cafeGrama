ICE CREAM

Strawberry Ice Cream Sandwich

Whole strawberries in each sandwich full of flavor. Chilled to optimal level to bring out the best flavor possible. Enjoy it now with your friends and delight in its flavor and sight.

Mango Ice Cream

This ice cream has a rich and creamy texture, sweetness and the flavors we all love and enjoy in mangoes. Looks and tastes refreshing best paired with a book and a quiet place.

CAKE

Chocolate Mousse

It is rich, chocolaty and decadent, hard not to fall in love with at the first bite. Best for any occassion and has a deep great flavor that will surely not leave any crumbs behind if it is eaten with a group of friends.

A great pair with a cup of coffee this chocolate crumb cake can double as abrunch or enjoy it as a casual after dinner cake. It has a great deep flavor and is also springy and soft.
